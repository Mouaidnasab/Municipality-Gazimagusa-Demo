Who is

Gazimağusa Municipality Mayor?
Dr. Süleyman Uluçay, who has been serving as the Mayor of Gazimağusa since December 25, 2022, was born in Balıkesir village, which is located in the Lefkoşa region of Cyprus, in 1962. Due to the inter-communal conflicts in Cyprus, the Uluçay family and the other Turkish Cypriot Family migrated from Balıkesir. Uluçay family migrated first to Cihangir and then to Gazimağusa.
